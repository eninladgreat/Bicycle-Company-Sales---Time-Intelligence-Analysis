# Bicycle-Company-Sales---Time-Intelligence-Analysis

This is a technical and analytical documentation for the Bicycle Company Sales Time Intelligence project in Power BI. It extends the sales data model with advanced DAX calculations, enabling year-over-year growth, month-over-month trends, rolling averages, seasonality, and YTD tracking.

This document provides comprehensive technical and analytical documentation for the "Bicycle Company Sales — Time Intelligence Analysis" project, developed using Microsoft Power BI Desktop and DAX Time Intelligence.  The analysis spans July 2022 to August 2025 and was last refreshed in February 2026. The project focuses on advanced temporal analysis, including year-over-year growth, month-over-month trends, rolling averages, seasonality detection, and year-to-date performance tracking. 

Key Highlights:

•	Gross Revenue: $77.55M 
•	Net Profit: $999.49K 
•	Net Profit Margin: 1.31% 
•	Year-on-Year Growth: $35.13M 
•	Profit Growth vs Prior Year: -$240.63K (negative due to cost pressures) 
•	Sales Growth Rate: 83% 

Data Model:

•	Star Schema: One fact table (Sales) and four dimension tables (Salesperson, Date, Products, Region). 
•	Calendar Table: Created using DAX for Time Intelligence calculations, enabling advanced analysis like YoY, MoM, and rolling averages. 

Key Findings:

1.	Revenue Growth: Exceptional growth from $7.8M in 2022 to $35.1M in 2024, with an 83% sales growth rate. 
2.	Profitability Concerns: Net profit margin is critically low at 1.31%, with profits declining despite rising revenue. 
3.	Seasonality: Peak sales months are August, November, September, and May, while troughs occur in June, July, and January. 
4.	Margin Destroyers: Products like Mountain Bike Socks and Classic Vest L consistently erode profit margins. 
5.	Regional Performance: The U.S. dominates sales, with five territories contributing significantly. 

Strategic Recommendations:

1.	Margin Recovery Plan: Conduct a 90-day review targeting a minimum 5% net margin. 
2.	Eliminate Margin-Destroying Products: Discontinue or adjust pricing for low-margin products. 
3.	Seasonal Inventory Strategy: Pre-position inventory and launch marketing campaigns ahead of peak months. 
4.	Sustain Revenue Momentum: Protect top-performing salespeople, products, and regions. 
5.	Dashboard Expansion: Add real-time margin alerts, salesperson leaderboards, and rolling forecasts for executive decision-making. 

Technical Details:

•	Tools Used: Microsoft Power BI, DAX, Power Query. 
•	Data Quality: 100% valid across all tables and columns. 
•	DAX Measures: 7 base measures and 16 advanced Time Intelligence measures. 
•	Dashboards: Four interactive Power BI pages for trend analysis, profitability tracking, and strategic insights.
